Data Science Careers 2020 to 2023:

Exploring Experienced-Based Pay Trends

Despite as one might think, data science positions also got impacted with COVID-19 and there are distinctive parameters that can be explored to observe the impact of each on both relative and absolute mean salaries across the world. The following interactive chart is yours to be played around with and make your own inferences.

Help yourself out with the three containers given on top of the chart to use the radio buttons in selecting parameters and see how the chart changes.

Note that you can access further information regarding mean salaries per experience level in data science careers as you hover over the chart datapoints throughout the years.

Navigation Information:

  • The Previous button refers to getting back to the start of the non-interactive story-telling.
  • The Home button takes you to the start of the webpage, a background information and the starting point of exploring the designed roadmap.
  • The Next button is designed to assist you in continuing the following scenes where you will receive more information related to different experience levels in data-related positinos.
